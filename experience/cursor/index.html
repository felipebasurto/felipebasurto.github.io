# Cursor Community (Madrid)

I am the **Cursor Madrid Ambassador** (since ~May 2025). This page is a visual archive of what we have run in the city. Full threads and comments stay on LinkedIn — recent activity.

  1. ~Mar 2026

    2nd Cursor Meetup (talks format)

    Partners **Tinybird** and **Kfund**. Back to the classic meetup shape: talks, learnings, and hallway conversations.

    • **getshell.ai** (Jose Miguel Avila Gomez, Francisco Novella Fletcher, Jose Manuel Jaén Lorites) — OpenClaw and bringing that stack to more people.
    • **David Gomes** (Cursor) — working inside Cursor day to day, **Cloud Agents**, and what is shipping fast (incl. long runs — see cursor.com/onboard).
  2. ~Feb 2026

    Café Cursor (first edition)

    All-day **casual coworking** at a café: people shipped their own projects, met other Cursor users, and talked shop — no talk track, no demos.

    Venue **eito** with three zones (main workspace, quieter back, upstairs hub). Coffee credits that people actually used up.

    **Three Cursor engineers** on-site all day — debugging and Q&A in real time.

  3. ~Dec 2025

    Cursor Hackathon × HappyRobot × Kibo Ventures

    Challenge: integrate a **HappyRobot AI agent** into a working product using Cursor.

    **Winners**

    1. **TravelBot** — corporate travel end to end (stays, transport, dining).
    2. **MedTracker** — automated daily calls to elderly relatives for medication check-ins.
    3. **Santa's Call** — Santa calls your kid about behaviour and wish lists.

    Next format teaser: **coffee**.

  4. ~Nov 2025

    First Cursor Hackathon (Madrid)

    Partners **Enzo Ventures**, **OneCoWork**, **productmakers.ai**. Build-first from minute one; venue filled instantly and a long waitlist.

    Theme: **gamification** — **~2 hours** to ship with Cursor.

    **Winners**

    1. **RoomieScore** — chores, expenses, and co-living for roommates.
    2. **FocoQuest** — productivity as missions and progression.
    3. **FinPlay** — personal finance made visual.

    Side story: someone working at a café nearby walked in and joined — **ended up on the winning team**.

  5. ~Jul 2025

    First official Cursor meetup (Madrid)

    Hosted at **Google for Startups Madrid** with support from **Takos** and volunteers who presented projects.

    Thanks to **Juan Bautista Martinez** and **Ben Lang** (Cursor) for the global community vision. Follow-up: survey + Madrid Cursor community link emailed to attendees.

  6. ~Jun 2025

    Cursor Ambassador

    Public commitment to run **meetups, workshops, and activities** in Madrid, stay active on Twitter / forums, and keep DMs open for collaboration ideas.
