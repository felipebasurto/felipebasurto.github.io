# Triple Check

**Triple Check** is a **pop rock** band in **Spanish** from **Burgos**: three of us (Felipe, Miguel, and Diego) who have known each other since we were kids. In **2020** we started writing and recording in earnest—self-produced releases, a growing catalog, and a sound we keep shaping release by release.

## The music

We’re guitar-driven, melody-first, and very much in the world of **radio-friendly Spanish pop rock**—songs you can turn up in a car or at a show. Some tracks are built for big singalongs; others lean more introspective, but the thread is the same: honest hooks and production we stand behind.

**EP *Atentamente, Triple Check* (2023)** pulls together a few of the songs that best represent that phase. Around it, we’ve been putting out **singles** and letting the setlist and the streams tell us what lands.

## Spotify (by the numbers)

These are **all-time streams** on Spotify, taken from the artist catalog (singles, EP, and the versions of each track as they live in the app). As of the last export, the project is past **1.7M** all-time plays; the most-streamed individual track to date is ***Ese Bar***, with several other songs in the **six figures**.

That growth has been **organic**—we’re not a label campaign, we’re three people learning how to write, record, and share music over time. The numbers are a side effect of consistency and of listeners who keep the songs in rotation.
